Welcome to the HTML JavaScript Class Riddles Page

  1. What occurs once in a minute, twice in a moment, twice in a moment, and never in a thousand years?


    The letter "m."

  2. What has six faces, but does not wear makeup, and has twenty-one eyes, but cannot see?


    A dice.

  3. Before Mount Everest was discovered, what was the highest mountain on Earth?


    Mount Everest.

  4. What runs around the whole yard without moving?


    A fence.

  5. What has a foot on each side and one in the middle?


    A yardstick.

  6. What is in seasons, seconds, centuries and minutes but not in decades, years or days?


    The letter N

  7. Can you drop a full glass and spill no water?


    Yes, when the glass is full of milk.

  8. I have no life, but I can die, what am I?


    A battery.

  9. When the water comes down, when it rains, I go up. What am I?


    An umbrella?

  10. I never ask questions, but always answered, what am I?


    A doorbell.
